Site Plan

Site Name

The site name/domain is Demiurgic Dynamics. An optional domain name could be demiurgicdynamics.com or demiurgicdynamics.org. This name represents a fictional tech company that specializes in robotics and artificial intelligence.

Site Purpose

Site provides information about the company, their products, and a page for hiring new employees. There is a form to fill out to apply for an open position.

Scenarios

  • What does Demiurgic Dynamics do?
  • What is their mission statement?
  • How can I apply to work there?
  • What products do they sell?

Color Schema

As demonstrated on this site, making heavy use of blues and whites, along with a darker background color.

Typography

I'm going to use the Poppins and Roboto font families as it looks professional and futuristic.
